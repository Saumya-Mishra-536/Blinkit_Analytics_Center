# Blinkit Business Analytics Project

**End-to-End Data Analysis | Interactive Dashboards | Business Insights**

---

##  Project Overview

This project delivers a **comprehensive analytics solution for Blinkit**, covering:

* Customer behavior & segmentation
* Customer feedback & sentiment analysis
* Marketing & channel performance
* Inventory & operational efficiency

The project combines **data cleaning, KPI engineering, and interactive dashboards** to simulate real-world business intelligence workflows.

## Key Business Insights

* Inactive customers show **higher average order value** but lower engagement
* Delivery delays impact **customer satisfaction directly**
* Marketing channels perform almost evenly — suggesting optimization opportunity
* Inventory damage highlights **supply chain inefficiencies**
* Revenue distribution is skewed toward mid-value orders
